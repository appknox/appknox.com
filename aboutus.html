History

The idea behind Appknox was triggered by the discovery that majority of the apps in the app stores were vulnerable to security issues. While hackers were exploiting the open source Android operating system to find vulnerabilities and inject malware, security issues in iOS apps were not too far behind. This led to the idea of building a tool to help app owners secure their apps.

The company founded by Harshit Agarwal, Subho Halder and Prateek Panda has found over 1,000 threats and audited over 900 applications, including the ones developed by Facebook, Google, Microsoft, Skype, Yahoo and PayPal.

  • Overview

    Mobile apps are a fast developing disruptive technology that is changing the way we live our lives. While technology brings convenience, it also brings along destructive threats. Appknox is a cloud-based mobile app security solution that is dedicated to securing the mobile ecosystem around the world.

    Appknox helps businesses and developers detect security loopholes in their mobile apps and also suggest ways to fix them. We also help improve productivity by enabling developers to build secure mobile applications without compromising on performance or agility, and by helping them identify security issues that truly exist.

Our Mission

To outthink and outsmart unethical hackers that exploit different cyber channels, and to provide a safe operating environment for ecommerce businesses around the globe. Appknox aims at building a safe and secure mobile ecosystem for everyone.
