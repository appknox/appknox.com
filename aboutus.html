History

The idea behind Appknox was triggered by the discovery that the majority of the apps in the Android play store were vulnerable to security issues. Hackers were exploiting the open source Android operating system to find vulnerabilities and inject malware. This led to the idea of building a tool to help app owners secure their apps. The company founded by Harshit Agarwal, Subho Halder and Prateek Panda has audited over 900 applications, and found over 1,000 threats including the ones developed by Facebook, Google, Microsoft, Skype, Yahoo and PayPal.

  • Overview

    Mobile apps are a fast developing disruptive technology that is changing the way we live our lives. With conveniences in technology also come threats that destruct. Appknox is a cloud based mobile app security solution that is dedicated to securing ecommerce businesses around the world. Appknox helps maintain or increase a developer’s productivity, allowing them to rapidly secure mobile applications in an agile development environment with absolutely no impact to innovation.

Our Mission

To outthink and outsmart unethical hackers that exploit different cyber channels and provide a safe operating environment for ecommerce businesses around the globe.
